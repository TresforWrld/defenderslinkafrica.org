Advocacy & Campaigns

Creating an enabling environment for human rights defenders requires systemic change. Our Advocacy Program works at all levels to strengthen legal frameworks and protection mechanisms.

National Level

Working with governments to adopt protection laws and policies.

Regional Level

Engaging the African Union to strengthen continental frameworks.

International Level

Leveraging UN mechanisms and global partnerships.

Partner With Us on Advocacy
Core Service

Documentation & Monitoring

Evidence-based advocacy requires rigorous documentation. We systematically monitor and document violations against human rights defenders to support accountability and inform protection strategies.

In 2024, we documented over 200 cases across 15 African countries, contributing to 12 successful accountability outcomes.
