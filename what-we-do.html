Our Services

What We Do

Comprehensive protection and support services designed to safeguard human rights defenders and strengthen their capacity to create lasting change.

Our Approach

Holistic Support for Defenders

We understand that human rights defenders face diverse and complex challenges. Our integrated approach addresses their immediate security needs while building long-term capacity for sustainable impact. From emergency response to systemic advocacy, we provide comprehensive support at every level.

Core Service

Emergency Protection

When human rights defenders face immediate threats, every second counts. Our Emergency Protection Program provides rapid response support to defenders in crisis.

  • 24/7 Emergency Hotline
    Round-the-clock support for defenders facing imminent danger
  • Emergency Relocation
    Safe temporary relocation for defenders at severe risk
  • Legal Assistance
    Rapid access to experienced human rights lawyers
  • Medical Support
    Medical care and psychological first aid for traumatized defenders
  • Security Measures
    Physical and digital security support for immediate protection

Core Service

Capacity Building

Strong defenders create stronger movements. Our Capacity Building Program equips human rights defenders with the skills, knowledge, and tools they need to work safely and effectively.

Security Training

Physical, digital, and psycho-social security training tailored to defenders' specific contexts and risks.

Documentation Skills

Training in human rights documentation, evidence gathering, and secure information management.

Advocacy Training

Strategic advocacy skills for engaging national, regional, and international human rights mechanisms.

Organizational Development

Support for building resilient, sustainable organizations with strong governance and management.

Core Service

Advocacy & Campaigns

Creating an enabling environment for human rights defenders requires systemic change. Our Advocacy Program works at all levels to strengthen legal frameworks and protection mechanisms.

National Level

Working with governments to adopt and implement national protection laws and policies for human rights defenders.

Regional Level

Engaging the African Union and regional bodies to strengthen continental human rights frameworks and protection mechanisms.

International Level

Leveraging UN mechanisms and international partnerships to advance global standards for defender protection.

Partner With Us on Advocacy
Core Service

Documentation & Monitoring

Evidence-based advocacy requires rigorous documentation. We systematically monitor and document violations against human rights defenders to support accountability and inform protection strategies.

In 2024, we documented over 200 cases of violations against human rights defenders across 15 African countries, contributing to 12 successful accountability outcomes.

  • Incident Documentation: Comprehensive recording of threats, attacks, and violations against defenders
  • Trend Analysis: Regular reports analyzing patterns and emerging threats to defenders
  • Database Management: Secure, searchable database of documented cases for research and advocacy
  • Public Reporting: Annual and thematic reports raising awareness of defender protection needs

Core Service

Psychosocial Support

Human rights work takes a toll. Our Psychosocial Support Program addresses the mental health and wellbeing needs of defenders, helping them sustain their important work without sacrificing their health.

Individual Counseling

One-on-one sessions with trained counselors experienced in trauma and human rights contexts.

Group Support

Peer support groups connecting defenders with others who understand their experiences.

Wellness Workshops

Training in stress management, self-care, and resilience-building techniques.

Family Support

Support services for defenders' families who also face stress and security concerns.

Specialized Service

Digital Security & Technology

In an increasingly digital world, online threats are as real as physical ones. Our Digital Security Program helps defenders protect themselves in the digital space.

Digital Security Audits

Comprehensive assessment of digital vulnerabilities and personalized security recommendations.

Secure Communications

Training and tools for encrypted communications and secure information sharing.

Data Protection

Support for secure data management, backup strategies, and protection against data breaches.

Incident Response

Rapid response to digital security incidents including hacking, surveillance, and online harassment.
