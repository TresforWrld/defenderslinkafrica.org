Who We Are

About DefenderLink Africa

A pan-African organization committed to protecting and empowering human rights defenders across the continent since 2014.

Our Story

Founded on Solidarity and Courage

DefenderLink Africa was born from the urgent need to protect those who courageously defend the rights of others. In 2014, a group of experienced human rights advocates came together with a shared vision: to create a dedicated organization that would serve as a lifeline for defenders facing threats across Africa.

Our founders had witnessed firsthand the risks faced by human rights defenders—journalists exposing corruption, lawyers fighting for justice, community activists protecting land rights, and women's rights advocates challenging harmful practices. They understood that without proper support systems, these brave individuals would continue to face intimidation, harassment, imprisonment, and even death.

Today, DefenderLink Africa has grown into a respected pan-African organization with regional offices in Kenya, South Africa, and Nigeria, and our headquarters in Lusaka, Zambia. We have supported over 500 human rights defenders across more than 25 countries, providing emergency assistance, training, and advocacy support.

Our Mission

To protect, support, and empower human rights defenders across Africa through comprehensive assistance, capacity building, and strategic advocacy, ensuring they can carry out their vital work safely and effectively.

Our Vision

An Africa where human rights defenders are protected, valued, and empowered to create just and equitable societies where all people can enjoy their fundamental rights and freedoms.

Our Values

The Principles That Guide Us

Our work is grounded in core values that inform every decision we make and every action we take.

01

Protection First

The safety and security of human rights defenders is our paramount concern. We respond swiftly to threats and provide ongoing support.

02

Human Dignity

We treat all individuals with respect, recognizing their inherent dignity. Our approach is grounded in empathy and cultural sensitivity.

03

Solidarity

We stand in solidarity with human rights defenders, amplifying their voices and joining their struggles for meaningful change.

04

Accountability

We maintain the highest standards of transparency and accountability, continuously measuring our impact and improving our work.

05

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from emergency response to capacity building, adopting best practices.

06

Independence

We maintain our independence from governments and political interests, guided solely by the needs of defenders and human rights principles.
